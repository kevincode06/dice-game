Instructions

  1. The game requires the players to roll the dice. The score will be determined from the sum of the outcome of the dice roll.
  2. If a die lands on 1, the player will automatically forfeit the points for that round because in order to score points, neither one of the die has to land on 1.
  3. To kickstart the game, players have to roll the dice and the outcome has to be a sum of 5.
  4. In the event that the dice lands on two number fives, the player will get a five-point headstart.
  5. The first player to reach fifty points wins!
  6. Happy Playing!
